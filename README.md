Welcome to the **United Kingdom Music Market Structure, Artist Diversity & Content Localization Analysis** project.  
This repository is part of my **Machine Learning Internship (Unified Mentor Private Limited)** and showcases a comprehensive dashboard and research on the UK Top 50 Playlist.

---

## 📌 Project Overview
The project explores:
- **Artist Dominance & Diversity**: Measuring concentration and unique artist counts.
- **Collaboration Structures**: Analyzing duet/group trends vs. solo tracks.
- **Content Explicitness**: Share of explicit vs. clean tracks.
- **Album vs. Single Releases**: Distribution of track types.
- **Track Duration Insights**: Interval-based analysis of song lengths.
- **Genre Prediction (ML)**: Using **OpenAI’s ViT Model** on album cover images to predict genres.
